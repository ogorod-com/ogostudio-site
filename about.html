About Ogo Studio

I'm Andrey. I run Ogo Studio on my own, alongside a full-time job and a family. I plan, design, and direct the work, and I build it with a lot of patience.

A geography game, and why it exists

When I was a kid, I played a small geography game on a family computer. I do not remember the name. I remember the feeling: matching flags to countries until the world stopped being a blur and became a place I knew. That feeling stayed with me.

Years later my oldest daughter, ten now, started asking me where countries were and why flags looked alike. GeoFlag came out of those questions. A geography game for curious players, built to make the world feel worth knowing.

Where this is going

GeoFlag is the first game, not the last. One person, building slowly, with the same care each time. The demo is on Steam now and the full game launches July 13, 2026. This is step one of a long road.
