Frequently asked questions

My progress was lost. Can I recover it?

GeoFlag saves all data locally on your device. If you cleared your browser or app data, the save may not be recoverable. We recommend keeping regular backups by exporting your profile when that feature becomes available.

The game won't launch or crashes on startup

Make sure your operating system is up to date. GeoFlag requires macOS 11+ or Windows 10+. If the issue persists, try reinstalling the application and contact us using the form below.

How do I earn more coins?

Coins are earned through gameplay — completing levels, achieving high accuracy, maintaining daily streaks, and unlocking achievements. There are no real-money purchases in GeoFlag.

I found incorrect geographic information

We take geographic accuracy seriously and follow UN naming standards. If you've spotted an error, please let us know using the form below and we'll investigate.
