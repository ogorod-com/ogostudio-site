Support

Having trouble, or want to reach me? Start here.

System requirements

GeoFlag runs on Windows 10 or later, 64-bit.

Where my save lives

Your save lives inside the GeoFlag app folder on your PC. As long as you don't uninstall the game or wipe Windows app data, your progress stays put. If you reinstall or move to a new PC, the save does not transfer automatically.

How do I earn more coins?

Coins are earned through gameplay: completing levels, hitting high accuracy, keeping daily streaks, and unlocking achievements. There are no real-money purchases in GeoFlag.

I found incorrect geographic information

I take geographic accuracy seriously and follow UN naming standards. If you have spotted an error, please tell me using the form below and I will look into it.

How to reach me

Use the form below, email me at ogostudiogames@gmail.com, or find me on the Steam community group. I read everything and reply as soon as I can.
