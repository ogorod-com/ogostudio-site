Frequently asked questions

My progress was lost. Can I recover it?

Your save lives inside the GeoFlag app folder on your PC. As long as you don't uninstall the game or wipe Windows app data, your progress stays put. If you reinstall or move to a new PC, the save does not transfer automatically.

The game won't launch or crashes on startup

Make sure your operating system is up to date. GeoFlag requires Windows 10 or later. If the issue persists, try reinstalling the game and contact me using the form below.

How do I earn more coins?

Coins are earned through gameplay: completing levels, achieving high accuracy, maintaining daily streaks, and unlocking achievements. There are no real-money purchases in GeoFlag.

I found incorrect geographic information

I take geographic accuracy seriously and follow UN naming standards. If you've spotted an error, please let me know using the form below and I'll look into it.
