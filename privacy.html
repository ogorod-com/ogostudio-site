Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Ogo Studio ("we", "us", "our") developed GeoFlag and other games as educational desktop applications. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our games.

Information We Collect

Our games do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. They operate entirely offline on your device.

All game progress, player profiles, preferences, and statistics are stored locally on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your computer and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Steam Platform

If you purchased our game through Steam, your interaction with the Steam platform is governed by Valve Corporation's privacy policy. We do not receive personal information from Steam. Any Steam features such as achievements, leaderboards, or cloud saves are handled entirely by Steam's infrastructure under Valve's privacy policy, available at store.steampowered.com/privacy_agreement/.

Children's Privacy

Our games are designed as family-friendly educational experiences. Because we do not collect any personal information from any user, including children, no parental consent mechanisms are required under the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) or similar regulations.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data breach from our services. Your locally stored game progress is protected by your device's own security measures.

Third-Party Services

Our games do not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services. No cookies, device identifiers, or usage metrics are collected.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if future versions of our games introduce online features. Any changes will be reflected in an updated "Last Updated" date and communicated through game updates.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at ogostudiogames@gmail.com.
