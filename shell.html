Scroll test

Scroll down. Once the sentinel at the top of the hero leaves the viewport, the Wishlist button fades into the header bar. Scroll back up and it fades out again. With JavaScript disabled the button simply stays visible, which is the safe default.

This noise you faintly see across the canvas is an inline SVG fractal-noise overlay at five percent opacity. No image request, no library. It gives true black a printed, tactile feel rather than a flat digital void.

On a desktop pointer, a soft cyan spotlight follows the cursor inside this hero region only. It sits behind the text, never washing it out, and it is switched off entirely on touch devices and under reduced motion.

Headings are Space Grotesk. Body is DM Sans. Hex values, build IDs, and timestamps are JetBrains Mono. All three families are self-hosted, so the page makes zero external network requests.

Spacing follows a four pixel base. Borders are hairline. Elevation comes from background brightness, never from shadow. The single accent is cyan. The beet palette belongs to the mascot, never to type.

When the real pages arrive, this shell gets deleted. Until then it is the proving ground for the foundation everything else stands on.
